Usage

To use candy buttons in your website, simply drop the stylesheet into your document's <head>, and add the class "candy-btn" to an element, along with any of the color names.
<head>
  <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://rmanaf.github.io/candy-btn/latest/candy-btn.min.css">
</head>

In the body:

<a href="#" class="candy-btn candy-purple candy-active">Button</a>
